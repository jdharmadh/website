data structures

I like testing theoretical algorithms on big datasets. Particularly, I'm interested in sublinear algorithms, streaming algorithms, and sketching.
Probabilistic Sketches Filters, HyperLogLog, CountMin
Solving problems like keeping a counter or marking distinct elements is straightforward, unless you're Google or Meta and the number of data points you have is in the billions. How do you run algorithms designed for arrays when all of your input doesn't even fit in memory? I provide toy solutions that solve these problems by sketching, i.e. sacrificing a small amount of accuracy for tons of efficiency. Take a look here.
Augmented Trees BinHeap, vEB Tree, Interval Tree
Binary search trees are a data structure for dictionary problems. However, if we make some small modifications to how they store data, we can solve a much wider range of problems efficiently. Take a look here.
